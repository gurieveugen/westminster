Westminster Day Surgery is an independent stand-alone day surgery unit situated 10 kilometres from the central business district of Perth in the suburb of Westminster. With two fully equipped theatres, an open access endoscopy service and 4 overnight licensed beds, Westminster Day Surgery offers an extensive range of clinical specialties and services.

Westminster Day Surgery is committed to providing safe, high quality health care to meet the needs of its clients and make their stay as comfortable as possible. Our facility is licensed by the WA Department of Health and has implemented and maintains a quality management system in accordance with International and National Quality Standards, ensuring only the highest standard of patient care is provided by our staff.
