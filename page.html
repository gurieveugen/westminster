Accreditation and Licensing

Westminster Day Surgery is accredited to the National Safety & Quality Health Service Standards and operates a Quality Management System that complies with the requirements of ISO 9001: 2008.

The hospital is approved and licensed annually by the Government of Western Australia Department of Health, under the Hospitals and Health Services Act 1927.
